Why Bella

Case acceptance turns on one conversation — and most teams never get to practice it.

Treatment coordinators are the biggest lever on production, yet they learn by shadowing, on real families, with the revenue on the line. Bella moves the reps off your patients.

Realistic, not helpful

Bella plays the parent who pushes back, stalls, and worries about cost — not an AI that gently lets your coordinator win. The friction is the point.

Scored on your method

Every session is graded against your framework — discovery, presenting, handling fear, closing — with specific feedback, not a number in isolation.

Managers see the lift

Track who’s improving and where the gaps are across the team, without ever exposing a raw transcript. Private by design.
